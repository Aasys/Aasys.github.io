Let’s start with a few things about me. I love problem solving through hardware and software solutions. I like exploring existing technologies, system architectures and software development tools. Most of the time you will find me breaking electronics, coding or lost in my own thoughts.

I love developing solutions and designing detailed system architecture and blueprints with great attention to abstraction and modularization. I see coding as a tool to help solve problems, and as such hold little preference for any systems or programming languages, rather I believe each problem has different affinities to different solutions. So, I like to keep myself up to date on new technologies.

I hold strong and abstract understanding of systems and programming, allowing me to quickly adapt to any new environment. I have a wide range of experience in different fields and technologies. Some things I have been involved are full stack web development, distributed systems, cloud computing, embedded systems, IOT, motion capture systems, web security, machine learning, CAD, 3D modeling and printing, and haptics research.

What am I up to currently? Most of my time goes to my personal and senior design project – Seamless Compute. You can read more about it in “Project Highlight” section.
